This folder is created to study the KEK Linac including polarization studies, the introduction report t is "Linac_Polarization Report".
Linac.txt is the data which records the elements, their position and the spin motion in the Linac. 
Vertical_bend.txt records all the vertical bending magnets in the Linac.
Linac.ipynb reads the data. 
Wien_Filter.ipynb is developed to calculate the paramters of the Wien_Filter, and describe the math and physics behind it.
